About Our School

Since 2026

Growing Fast with Love and Care

Ramakrishna Vidyamandir is a rapidly growing preschool established in 2026. In just one year, we have proudly nurtured a family of over 37 excellent learners.

We believe in a holistic approach to early education. Our curriculum is designed to not just educate, but to allow children to grow while staying connected with nature. Our open grounds provide the perfect space for students to play, explore, and stay grounded.

"Education is the manifestation of the perfection already in man." - Swami Vivekananda

Why Choose Us?

We offer a unique blend of modern education and traditional values in a nurturing environment.

Specialised Teachers

We have dedicated and specialised teachers for extracurricular activities like Drawing and Yoga to ensure all-round development.

Nature Connected

Our school is open and close to nature. We have ample open space for students to play, run, and stay in touch with the ground.

Transport Facility

Safe and reliable transport is available. We pick up students from Maitha, Majdiha, Kuchishol, Nikunjapur, Gamidya and neighboring villages.
